BOOKS

A Year with the Seals

By Alix Morris, Foreword by Sy Montogmery
Advance Reader Copy

A book that blends the history of seal's near-extinction, captivating stories of human-seal relationships, and Alix's own experience of spending a year with the seals. Even there's a lot of terms and ideas, the author still maintain the logical flow that's easily followed by readers, and the stories that she told were truly extraodrinary.

世界上最透明的故事

作者：杉井光；譯者： 簡捷
2025年8月購自台南政大書城

少接觸推理小說這個類別，覺得故事線不是令此書成功的原因，編排伏筆等都不難看透，作者文筆一般。本書反而是因為閱讀體驗獨特而成為暢銷書。這方面只能透過閱讀實體書才有辦法了解，無法劇透。

歡迎加入動物聊天室

作者：山極壽一、鈴木俊貴；譯者： 張東君
2026年5月購自博客來

本書是研究猩猩的山極教授和研究鳥類的鈴木教授之間的對談。內容深入淺出地探討動物行為、叫聲背後的溝通機制，並按主題分為不同的短篇。因為行文簡單易懂且篇章主題鮮明，即使背景知識不多也能容易看懂。

賺錢買維他奶 (La Dolce Vitasoy)

作者：筆華棋
絕版書

原高登連載小說。內容講述一個執絝子弟因為找工作的問題跟父母爭吵，後來去便利店兼職而重燃對夢想的熱情的故事。行文不算特別流暢，文本偶有錯字，但不減故事張力，有鼓勵人堅持熱愛的事物、去努力追夢的效果。

MOVIES

Disclosure Day

Directed by Steven Spielberg
AMC Kips Bay 15

A movie about disclosing the existence of ETs to the public — another classic ET movie. Too much chasing in the beginning that tries to build suspense, but only minimal effect and it got boring. By the end the broadcasting scene was touching. The movie would have been more engaging if the visual design of the ET and the storyetlling is more creative.

Star Wars: The Mandalorian and Grogu

Directed by Jon Favreau
AMC Orpheum 7

First Star Wars movie in the theatres in my life!! Excellent visual and audio effects, but the story feels more than a whole season put onto the big screens, than a full movie. I'm so glad to see Grogu's growth and his chemistry with Din Djarin, can't wait the moment when Grogu learns to talk!

The Sheep Detectives

Directed by Kyle Balda
AMC Orpheum 7

A mystery movie that carries the theme of human-sheep kinship, and the main characters are sheep! The storyline is interesting, filled with British humor, that gave me a great laugh. Highly recommended if you're looking for something light~!
